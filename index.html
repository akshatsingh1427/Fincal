What is SIP?

Invest a fixed amount every month regardless of market conditions. Small habit, massive wealth over time.

M = P × [((1+r)ⁿ – 1) / r] × (1+r)
🌱

Power of Compounding

Your returns earn returns. Starting at 25 vs 35 — the 10-year head start can double your final corpus.

A = P × (1 + r/n)^(n×t)
⚖️

NAV & Units

Net Asset Value = per-unit price of a fund. ₹5000 at NAV ₹50 = 100 units. More units = more wealth.

Units = Amount ÷ NAV
🎯

Rupee Cost Averaging

SIP buys more units when markets fall, fewer when they rise — naturally averaging your cost over time.

Avg Cost = Total ₹ ÷ Total Units
🏷️

Expense Ratio

Annual fund fee. 1% vs 0.5% on ₹10L over 20 years can cost ₹5L+. Always compare before investing.

Net Return = Gross – Expense %
📊

CAGR

Compound Annual Growth Rate — the steady yearly rate your investment would have grown. Best comparison metric.

CAGR = (End/Start)^(1/n) – 1
